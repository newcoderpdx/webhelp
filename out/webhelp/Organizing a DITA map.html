Organizing a DITA map

This brief series of steps will who you how to organize your DITA topics.

When you first create your DITA map, it will be empty. The topicref href is used to insert your topics into a map.
Note: Make sure all of your topic files are in the same folder.
  1. Click on the Text tab. (It is slightly more complicated to perform these tasks in Author view).
  2. Type <topicref href="">
    Your linked topics will automatically come up.
  3. Choose a topic.
  4. Make sure to add your end </topicref> tag.
  5. Add other topics as desired.
  6. To nest a topic inside another, simply put its <topicref>inside it.
    <topicref href="main.dita" ><topicref href="subtopic.dita"></topicref></topicref>
    You will now have an organized map of DITA topics.
